FAQ’s?

What is Zenvi?

Zenvi is a mental health app designed to support individuals battling anxiety, depression, and stress. It offers personalized messages, calming tools, and progress tracking to help you on your journey to better mental health.

Is Zenvi free to use?

Yes! Zenvi offers a free version with access to daily motivational messages, calming widgets, and progress tracking. However, for a deeper experience, Zenvi Premium offers advanced features.

How does Zenvi personalize my experience?

When you sign up, Zenvi asks you a few questions about your mental health goals and challenges. Based on your responses, it tailors motivational messages, reminders, and tools to fit your needs.
